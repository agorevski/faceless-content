## Data Sources

### Reddit
- r/TrueCrime
- r/UnresolvedMysteries
- r/serialkillers
- r/TrueCrimeDiscussion

### Other Sources
- **Wikipedia** - Case summaries, timelines, and verified facts
- **Court Records** - PACER for federal cases, state court databases
- **News Archives** - Newspapers.com, Google News Archive
- **Podcasts** - Serial, Crime Junkie, My Favorite Murder for case leads
- **Documentary Databases** - IMDb for true crime documentaries
- **FBI Vault** - Declassified case files
- **The Charley Project** - Missing persons database
- **NamUs** - National Missing and Unidentified Persons System

- Always verify facts from multiple sources before scripting
- Be respectful of victims and their families
- Avoid sensationalizing violence
