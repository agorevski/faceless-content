## Proven Viral Formats (Apply to Any Niche)

### Hook Templates That Work

1. **"The Truth About..."** - Myth-busting, creates curiosity
2. **"I Tried X For 30 Days"** - Personal experiment format
3. **"Ranking X From Worst to Best"** - Opinion + debate bait
4. **"What They Don't Tell You About..."** - Insider knowledge
5. **"X vs Y - Which Is Actually Better?"** - Comparison debates
6. **"How [Famous Person] Really..."** - Celebrity connection
7. **"The [Number] Types of..."** - Categorization content
8. **"Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong"** - Contrarian takes
9. **"What Happens If You..."** - Consequence exploration
10. **"The Real Reason..."** - Hidden explanation content

## Algorithm-Friendly Variations

### High Retention Formats

- **Cold Open Hook**: Start with the most shocking moment
- **Countdown Format**: "5...4...3..." builds anticipation
- **Mystery Reveal**: Withhold key info until end
- **Before/After**: Transformation content
- **Tier Lists**: Ranking creates debate = comments

### Comment Bait Phrases

- "Let me know if I missed anything..."
- "Part 2 if this gets [X] likes"
- "Do you agree with #3?"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Wrong answers only..."

## Quick Wins (Fastest to Produce)

These formats tend to perform well and are quick to make:

1. **Scary Stories:** "True Scary Stories from Reddit" compilations - just need good narration
2. **Finance:** "X Money Mistakes" - listicle format, easy to script
3. **Luxury:** "Why X Costs So Much" - research-based, visually interesting

## Suggested Starting Schedule

### Week 1-2: Build Foundation

- Produce 2-3 videos per niche (6-9 total)
- Focus on evergreen topics
- Test what resonates

### Week 3-4: Analyze & Adjust

- Check analytics
- Double down on best performers
- Experiment with thumbnails

### Month 2: Scale What Works

- Increase frequency for winning niche
- Consider dropping underperformer
- Start building series/franchises
